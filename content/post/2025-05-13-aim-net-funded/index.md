Great news from our team! 🎉

The **Computer Vision and Pattern Discovery (CVPD) group** at the **University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU)** is part of **AIM-Net — _AI-Assisted Multiscale Imaging Network for Cellular and Molecular Processes_**, a national research network funded in the **Redes de Investigacion 2024** call of the **Ministerio de Ciencia, Innovacion y Universidades (MICIU)**.

<!--more-->

The official funding resolution for the call was published on **13 May 2025**, and the network formally started on **1 September 2025**.

AIM-Net brings together leading Spanish groups working at the intersection of **microscopy**, **quantitative biology**, and **artificial intelligence** to address one of the field's key challenges: integrating imaging data across scales, from molecules and cells to tissues, and across different microscopy modalities. By combining experimental and computational expertise, the network aims to develop AI-based strategies for data integration, interpretation, and modeling in bioimaging.

Within this initiative, the **CVPD group** contributes its expertise in **bioimage analysis**, **computer vision**, and **deep learning**. Our participation in AIM-Net strengthens collaborations with national partners and helps advance joint activities in research, training, open science, and community building around AI-driven multiscale imaging. 🚀

The network is funded under grant **RED2024-153844-T**, funded by **MICIU/AEI/10.13039/501100011033**.
