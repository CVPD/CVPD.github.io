We are pleased to share that the **CALM4GRAINS subproject — _Cross-domain Adaptation and Learning for Microscopy_ — has been officially funded** by the **Spanish Ministry of Science, Innovation and Universities (MICIU)** under the **Research Projects in the field of Artificial Intelligence 2025 (AIA 2025)** call.

Led by the **CVPD** group at the **University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU)**, CALM4GRAINS is **Subproject 2** of the coordinated **GRAINS** initiative (*Green Responsible AI for Next-gen Sustainable Bio-Imaging*). The project will develop cross-domain adaptation and learning methods for microscopy, with a focus on robust transferability, reusable representations, and computational efficiency.

The subproject, which runs from **2025 to 2028**, has been awarded **328,500 EUR** in funding.

We look forward to contributing to greener and more transferable AI methods for biomedical microscopy within the broader GRAINS coordinated project.
