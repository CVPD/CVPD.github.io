**Fatemeh Keneshlou** is a PhD student at the University of the Basque Country. She is currently pursuing a PhD under the supervision of [Dr. Fadi Dornaika](https://cvpd.github.io/author/fadi-dornaika/) and [Dr. Ignacio Arganda-Carreras](https://github.com/iarganda) within the TOSBI (Towards Scalable and Generalized Solutions in Biomedical Images) project, a major research initiative at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU).
She earned an MSc in Biomedical Engineering from AmirKabir University of Technology, Iran, in 2024 and a BSc in Materials Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology, Iran, in 2020. Her multidisciplinary background combines engineering principles with advanced computational methods, providing a strong foundation for research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biomedical sciences.
